FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What types of cakes do you offer?

We offer a wide variety of cakes, including classic flavors like chocolate and vanilla, as well as specialty cakes like red velvet and carrot cake. We also have seasonal options and can accommodate custom cake requests.

Do you provide gluten-free or vegan options?

Yes, we understand the importance of catering to dietary preferences and restrictions. We offer delicious gluten-free and vegan cake options that maintain the same great taste and quality as our regular cakes.

Can I place a custom cake order for a special occasion?

Absolutely! We love creating custom cakes for special occasions. Whether it's a birthday, wedding, or any other celebration, our talented bakers can bring your vision to life. Just let us know your requirements, and we'll work together to create a memorable cake.

What coffee specialties do you serve?

Our coffee menu features a range of specialty options to satisfy every coffee lover's cravings. From classic espresso-based drinks like cappuccinos and lattes to indulgent flavored coffees and refreshing iced beverages, we have something for everyone.

Do you offer delivery or takeout services?

Yes, we offer convenient delivery and takeout services for your convenience. You can enjoy our delicious cakes and coffee from the comfort of your own home or office. Simply place your order online or give us a call, and we'll take care of the rest.
